This is a 1 bar (or candlestick) ahead price level predictor for orders. Instructions and explaination below chart.

Chart Instructions

Select an area to zoom in. Home icon at top right will reset both axes. Click legend items at right to remove and restore traces. The black line (zz) is a simple zigzag indicator - normally not predictive. It will be replaced with a green/red line showing actual trades soon. Note: hpred, lpred, cpred, mpred are 1 bar ahead predictions of high, low, close, and mid respectively. hpred and lpred will set order price levels - click on the legend to have them drawn. Also take a look at cpred (red) mpred (green) crossings to see advance indication of multiple day direction change (trend). I will add candlesticks instead of bars as an option also at some point. I prefer bars.
The LB= stands for LastBar. Make sure it is the last trading day as I have to manually kick off fetch data and run predictions right now. I may not update charts every day as I am developing. I will automate that down the road.
